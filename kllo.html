PRIME OBJECTIVE

The primary goal of this app is to provide groups with the ability to reduce the amount of irrelevant suggestions and to quickly come to a consensus. An additional goal is to create an anonymous platform in which individuals can feel comfortable to voice their opinions and not feel the social anxiety that comes with deciding for the whole group.

Pix! isn’t a typical exploration app where you are presented a lengthy list of recommendations and hotspots, but more so it focuses on getting the user(s) to quickly decide on something and spend more time doing and less time searching.


kllo. LOGO

I've decided on the name 'Pix!'. I chose this name because of it's simple and sounds like the word 'picks'. I chose to go with a pixie/fairy as my product icon because I wanted this app to feel like a guide, or companion that works its ~magic~ to provide a solution. The idea of Pix! was strongly inspired by the pixie guide in Zelda: Phantom Hourglass, as it was based off the pixie that guided Link around. I also think a pixie is a unique visual representation instead of the typical location pin, map, or plane icons, that also added to the whimsical element of the app.
