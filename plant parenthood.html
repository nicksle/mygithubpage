The Problem

Traveling as a group is more fun, but how might we make group decision making more harmonious?

Everyone enjoys traveling. The experience of seeing [] places, eating [] foods, and trying new activities through traveling is a [] that every person wants to enjoy; but the experience is even better when you have someone to share it with.


Because of this, people often like to travel in groups when doing road trips or traveling to a new place; but although a larger group means more fun, it also means more disputes when it comes to making decisions: from what music to play in the car or where everyone wants to eat for dinner.
